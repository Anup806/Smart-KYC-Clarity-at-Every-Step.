A) Title (for YouTube/Drive)
Smart KYC: Clarity at Every Step. - eSewa x WWF Hackathon 2026 Registration Demo (Team BUG)

B) Description (2-4 lines)
A 60-90 second registration demo for Challenge 4: Intelligent KYC Experience.
We show a smart KYC stepper with OCR auto-fill (mocked), validation, and status transparency.
Goal: reduce form errors and KYC support tickets.

0:00-0:08
KYC is where users get stuck: small errors and unclear status create support tickets.

0:08-0:18
We are Team BUG, and this is Smart KYC: Clarity at Every Step.

0:18-0:35
Step one, Personal Info and Address. Inline checks catch typos early.
Step two, Document Upload. OCR auto-fills fields and quality checks flag blurry or incomplete files.

0:35-0:55
Next, Review and Submit. The user sees all data, fixes issues, and submits once.
Then the Status Tracker shows where KYC stands with a simple timeline.

0:55-1:10
If something goes wrong, Smart KYC: Clarity at Every Step. shows actionable rejection reasons and contextual help,
so users fix the exact issue instead of opening a ticket.

1:10-1:25
This MVP is built in Streamlit and Python, with OCR mocked for demo and EasyOCR planned.
We target 35 percent fewer errors and 25 percent fewer support tickets.
Thanks for considering Team BUG.
